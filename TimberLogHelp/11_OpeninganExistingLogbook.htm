Opening an Existing Logbook

To open an existing Logbook from within TimberLog, open the application and from the Start Screen select ‘Open Logbook’ at the lower left. The right side of the screen will change to a display similar to Windows Explorer on which you can navigate to the folder where your Logbook is located. If you single-click on a filename, the bottom of the screen will display the Logbook details. If you double-click on the filename, the Logbook Window will open with your selected Logbook.

 

You can also open an existing Logbook directly from Windows Explorer by double-clicking on the filename or by right-clicking on the filename and selecting ‘Open’.
